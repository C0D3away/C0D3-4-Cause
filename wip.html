How cryptocurrency should be, and what it should be used for...

The future of software is in digital currency. Software developers could sell their software for digital currency, and exchange that digital currency for cash. The software customers could ask a developer to make him a software he needs, and the dev will sell the customer the software.


The customers earn cryptocurrency by mining. About every hour the user earns .01dc (digital currency). Over time he could build up enough dc to buy software. 1dc = 1$. So every 10hrs the user gets a dollar in dc. Video games could be sold in dc, therefore forcing gamers to play the game until they have enough dc to buy another one.
